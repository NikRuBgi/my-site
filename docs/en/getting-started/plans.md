# Subscription Plans

Dybee is available in two plans. Features in this documentation are marked where a specific plan is required.

## Energy Plan

Covers stages 1 and 2 of the implementation methodology.

**Included features:**

- Building configuration and user management
- Energy source setup and invoice management
- Baseline modeling and savings calculation (IPMVP)
- Portfolio-level building comparison
- Dashboards, widgets, and reports
- Project documentation (logbook, comments, tasks)

## Analytics Plan

Covers all four stages. Includes everything in the Energy Plan, plus:

- BMS integration via Dybee-Link (BACnet)
- Real-time data acquisition and point mapping
- Digital twin modeling (entities, families, energy flows)
- Automated rules and fault detection
- KPIs and formula calculations
- Optimization hints

!!! info "Analytics Plan"
    Features that require the Analytics Plan are marked with this notice throughout the documentation.
